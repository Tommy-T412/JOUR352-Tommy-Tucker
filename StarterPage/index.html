Overview

Yves Tumor is a psychadelic rock artist known for their alaborate music videos and innovative song writing.

Yves Tumor often dresses in drag in a similar vein to many 80s rock bands, but in a modern era.

They are probably the closest thing we have today to a genuine rock star.
